Lego Projects

This page serves to document the projects I have done to create play experiences and tell stories through the lego medium.

You can keep up to date with work in progress updates about my work via my instagram (@lilly.osaurus), Or you can experience my finished stories and creation showcases on my TikTok (@the_bricksmith) or my youtube channel (The Bricksmith).

O'five Pizzaria

A build which tells the story of a disabled monster hunter couple who open up a pizaria out of some old train cars.

Learn more

Rocky Beach Story

A project where I tell a short story from the perspective of a girl who washes up on a rocky beach in the middle of a storm. This also let me experiment with rock and wave building techniques.

Watch video telling the story

Ship Building experiement

A work in progress build where I wanted to tell the story of how a girl from one of my other builds ended up on a stormy beach. But served mostly as a way for me to experiment with building a strong boat hull.
